I've been involved in ministry since the age of 14. By God's grace, I served as a musician at our church (Drums, Bass, Keys), and later served our District Youth Ministries as Music Director and musician. After getting married to my lovely wife Lisa, I was asked to be the Youth Pastor of Elohim Christian Church in a Volunteer Role while I completed my Bible Education in 1997. In 2001, I moved to New Jersey to serve in my first full-time Youth/Worship Pastorate at Evangel Chapel. From there, we moved to Springfield, Missouri to study at the Assemblies of God Theological Seminary. In 2011, we planted New Walk Church in South Plainfield, NJ.

In 2018, I had the opportunity to record two Video Series for Right Now Media.
